Zyneon+ Help

What is Zyneon+?

Description

Zyneon+ is the simplest way to play optimized Minecraft.
It is a Minecraft modpack for the Fabric modloader and offers a bunch of features.

Play your single player worlds with friends, without a server

Thanks to the Essential and; or the e4mc mod, you can simply "convert" your singleplayer worlds to servers and play with your friends.

Boost your FPS!

Thanks to sodium, lithium and many more optimization mods, Zyneon+ runs at high FPS and still offers you numerous features.

Bye bye OptiFine!

We don't have OptiFine in the modpack because we rely on modern optimizations. However, we still support all the features that OptiFine has and much more!

Do you miss the console edition?

No problem!
Enjoy playing Zyneon+ with a controller, just as you know it!

Through ease to compatibility

Zyneon+ is built on Fabric, which is a modern and lightweight modloader. In addition, we do not add any new content directly into the game, which is why you can play with the modpack anywhere, whether vanilla PvP, vanilla RPG or classic vanilla servers.

Growing selection of versions

Zyneon+ is available for 1.8.9, 1.12.2, 1.16.5, 1.17.1, 1.18.2, 1.19.4, 1.20.6, 1.21.1 and continues to be updated to support more and more versions.
